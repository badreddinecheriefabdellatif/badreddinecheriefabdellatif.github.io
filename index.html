About me

I am a CNRS researcher (Chargé de Recherche) based at Sorbonne Université, in the Laboratoire de Probabilités, Statistique et Modélisation (LPSM).

Prior to that, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Statistics at the University of Oxford, and I received my PhD in 2020 from Institut Polytechnique de Paris, prepared in the Center for Research in Economics and Statistics (CREST), Paris, under the supervision of Pierre Alquier.

My research covers various aspects of mathematical statistics and machine learning. I have worked during my PhD on variational inference and robustness, and I am more generally interested in PAC-Bayes theory, generalization bounds, high-dimensional statistics, and in online, transfer & deep learning.

You can find here my PhD thesis.
