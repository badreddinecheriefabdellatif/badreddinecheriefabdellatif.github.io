About me

I am a PhD student in statistics at Center for Research in Economics and Statistics (CREST), Paris, under the supervision of Pierre Alquier. My main research interests lie in Variational Bayes inference for approximating intractable posteriors in statistics and machine learning. More precisely, my work focuses on the statistical properties of Variational Bayes procedures and the design of efficient algorithms for computing them. I also provide teaching sessions in mathematics at ENSAE ParisTech. Prior that, I graduated with the MVA Master program in machine learning and computer vision from Ecole Normale Supérieure Paris-Saclay and obtained the engineering degree of ENSAE ParisTech.
